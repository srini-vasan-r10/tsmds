About Us

Thiagarajar School of Management (TSM)

Established in 1962, Thiagarajar School of Management (TSM) is a renowned institution that provides professional education to managers and entrepreneurs. TSM offers AICTE-approved and NBA-accredited MBA and PGDM programs and has been conferred autonomous status by UGC. With a focus on practical training and industry connections, TSM emphasizes holistic education and soft skill development.

Doctoral Research SUMMIT 2025 (DS '25)

The Doctoral Research SUMMIT 2025 is an academic event designed to foster research excellence and collaboration among doctoral scholars. In collaboration with ICN Business School, France, the summit offers a platform for presenting research, receiving expert feedback, and participating in workshops and networking sessions. The focus is on interdisciplinary research, methodological advancement, and industry relevance.

Tracks and Themes

Track 1. Human Resource Sustainability

Focuses on how organizations can manage human resources to promote social and environmental sustainability, covering topics like green HRM, employee well-being, and ethical labor practices.

Track 2. Sustainable Finance

Examines financial strategies and instruments that support long-term economic and environmental health, including green banking, impact investing, and sustainable risk management.

Track 3. Sustainability and Operations Management

Explores the integration of sustainable practices into operational processes and supply chains, covering areas like sustainable logistics, lean manufacturing, and circular economy models.

Track 4. Sustainability & Governance

Focuses on the role of corporate governance and public policy in driving sustainability, including corporate social responsibility, stakeholder engagement, and ethical leadership.

Track 5. Digital Transformation & Technology for Sustainability

Highlights how digital technologies can be leveraged to achieve sustainability goals, such as using AI for resource optimization, blockchain for transparent supply chains, and big data for environmental monitoring.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the dates for the Doctoral Summit?

Registration fees are non-refundable. Please ensure all details are correct before completing your payment.
